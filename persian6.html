Imperative

The imperative only deals with two subjects: the second person singular and the second person plural.
The normal construction of the imperative in Persian is by adding a [bé-] prefix to the present stem,


I sleep = mi-khaab-am : the root is 'khaab', so the imperative is 'be-khaab!'


When the [bé-] prefix is followed by an [-o-] in the following syllable, its vowel changes to /ó/:


I eat= mi-khor-am : the root is 'khor', so the imperative is 'bo-khor!'
